About Us

Organic Me

Organic Me is businesses that specialize in producing and selling meat products that meet specific organic standards. Organic meat is produced without the use of synthetic pesticides, antibiotics, growth hormones, or genetically modified organisms (GMOs). Animals raised for organic meat are typically given access to the outdoors and are raised in more humane and environmentally sustainable conditions compared to conventional factory farming.

Our Farms

Organic Me company farms are known for their commitment to sustainable and environmentally responsible agriculture practices. These farms prioritize organic farming methods, which exclude the use of synthetic pesticides, antibiotics, and genetically modified organisms (GMOs). The animals on these farms are raised in humane and ethical conditions, with access to the outdoors and spacious living environments that promote their well-being. Organic meat company farms place a strong emphasis on animal welfare, avoiding the use of growth hormones and limiting the administration of antibiotics to ensure the health of the animals.

Our Meat

Meat is a common and popular food source derived from the flesh of animals, typically domesticated livestock such as cattle, pigs, chickens, and sheep. It is a significant part of many diets worldwide and is a rich source of essential nutrients, including protein, vitamins, and minerals like iron and zinc. Meat comes in various forms, including red meat (beef, lamb, and pork), poultry (chicken and turkey etc.)
