    About Us

    In 2012, our company was founded by Anna, Adrian, Armaan, Zaim and Zibby with the goal of creating beautiful and unique products while also promoting sustainability and reducing waste.

    The founders had a passion for upcycling and a belief that they could make a positive impact on the environment and society by reusing and repurposing old fabrics and clothing.
